# Limited Space (150 points)

Students are given an encoded message

.. -.-. - ..-.{... .--. .--.-. ._-.. ----- ...._-- .- - -. .-.}

Some space are missing, decode the given message and try to guess where is the missing space,

and retrieve the original message

. . -.-. - ..-.{... .--. .- -.-. ._-.. ----- . ..._-- .- - - . .-.}
